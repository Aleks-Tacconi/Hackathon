What is the radius of a circle?%%%%%The radius of a circle is the distance from the center of the circle to any point on its circumference. It is half the diameter of the circle.
How do I calculate the circumference of a circle?%%%%%To calculate the circumference of a circle, use the formula C = 2πr, where C is the circumference and r is the radius of the circle. Alternatively, if you have the diameter (d), you can use the formula C = πd, where π (pi) is approximately 3.14159.
