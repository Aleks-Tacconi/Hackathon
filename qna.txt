What is the radius of a circle?%%%%%The radius of a circle is the distance from the center of the circle to any point on its circumference. It is typically denoted as 'r'.
How do I calculate the circumference of a circle?%%%%%The circumference of a circle can be calculated using the formula C = 2πr, where C is the circumference and r is the radius of the circle. Alternatively, if you know the diameter (d), you can use the formula C = πd.
