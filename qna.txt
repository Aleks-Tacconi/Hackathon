What is the radius of a circle?%%%%%The radius of a circle is the distance from the center of the circle to any point on its circumference. It is typically denoted by the letter 'r' and is half the diameter of the circle.
How do I calculate the circumference of a circle?%%%%%The circumference of a circle can be calculated using the formula: C = 2πr, where C is the circumference and r is the radius of the circle, or C = πd, where d is the diameter of the circle.
